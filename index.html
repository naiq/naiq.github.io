Biography
  • Hi! I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the School of Data Science, Fudan University with Prof. Yanwei Fu.
  • I have completed my PHD study at the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, under the supervision of Prof. Xiangyang Xue. Previously, I received the Bachelor's degree from Xidian University, and majored in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics during my undergraduate study.
  • My research interests span computer vision, machine learning and deep learning. At present, I devote to the research of human understanding, model robust, image generation and computer-aided diagnosis.
  • Open-source projects can be found at GitHub, and publications can be found at Google Scholar. More details are in CV.
